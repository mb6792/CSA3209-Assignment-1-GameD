All About the GameD Group

We are a group of academics and researchers from the Faculty of Education and the Faculty of ICT at the University of Malta, who are interested in the frequency and use of digital and video games by the Maltese Population. With the help and support of the Malta Communications Authority (MCA), and the University's Corporate Research and Knowledge Transfer, we have embarked on this project, to determine how much digital and video games are popular with the Maltese households and the extent to which these types of games are played.

As a team, and as a research group - "gamED", we have decided to share our common interests and our different areas of expertise to be able to come up with project ideas that will help use digital and video games to educate, entertain and empower individuals in the Maltese society.
